About Us

Shri Balaji Enterprises:

Shree Balaji Enterprises is one of the esteemed organizations from Delhi that are active in the finance sector of India. The specializes in offering one of a kind service for a home loan, loan against property, business loan, auto loan, and personal loan, for which it has tied-up with a number of leading banks in the industry like Kotak Mahindra Bank, IDFC Bank, ICICI Bank, Tata Capital, etc. For over a decade, we have been facilitating people with different kinds of financial needs to acquire the right kinds of loan solutions through our strategic assistance. Establishment of Shree Balaji Enterprises took place in the year 2008, when two veterans viz., Mr. Gaurav Sharma and Mrs. Geetu Sharma planned to make an entrepreneurial foray in the finance sector. Ever since its inception, the company has served a number of corporate clients as well as individuals to procure different kinds of loans in a legitimate manner and with sheer ease.
